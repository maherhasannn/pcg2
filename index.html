So... what makes us different?

Piedmont's mission is to strengthen the community. That's why we donate 51% of our operating reserves every 6 months*.

We invest back into the community, ensuring that our work makes a lasting impact. With our elite team of hackers, designers, and visionaries, we take 6 projects a semester and don't stop until our clients are completely satisfied. Our small, focused teams deliver exceptional results by dedicating time and attention to each project. Piedmont stands out by combining innovation, excellence, and a commitment to making a difference in every project we take on.
